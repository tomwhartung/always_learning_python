Disclaimer

¹ We will come up with some sort of official disclaimer some day, for now, please just take things with a grain of salt.

To be perfectly honest, the main reason this quiz is online at this time is so I can get some feedback on it.

Most importantly, this quiz is a work in progress, and at this time I am not by any means ready to guarantee that it will give you an accurate result.

I've had a lot of fun writing this quiz! It's been a real challenge to come up with the questions without borrowing too heavily from Briggs-Meyers and Keirsey — and I love a good challenge! And I've had even more fun with writing the code to support having a different type of quiz. Note that it allows for a varying number of options (rather than just two either/or options) and even allowing visitors to opt-out some of the more experimental questions that appear in longer versions.

But it definitely needs more work!

If accuracy is important to you, please take other quizzes and get a variety of opinions. And if you really want to learn exactly what your four letter type is, by all means take the real MBTI® instrument!
